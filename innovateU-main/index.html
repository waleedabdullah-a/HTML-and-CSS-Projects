About the Event:

Welcome to a very special conference tailored for the next generation of IT leaders! This event is designed to inspire and equip IIUI students with the knowledge and insights they need to thrive in the dynamic intersection of emerging technologies and the job market. Our lineup of speakers, exclusively from the industry, will provide invaluable perspectives to help you navigate the exciting journey ahead.



Theme:

Tomorrow: Navigating the Intersection of Emerging IT Technologies

Message For Students:

Attending this conference can be the key to unlocking a promising career in IT for students. It offers a vibrant and immersive learning experience where students can engage with industry experts and gain the skills and confidence necessary to thrive in the fast-paced realm of emerging technologies. Let us help you become the future leaders of the tech industry!

What is Compulink?

CompuLink is a dynamic and immersive technology event hosted by the International Islamic University. It serves as a vibrant platform that brings together students, tech enthusiasts, and industry professionals to showcase their skills, creativity, and innovation in the field of software development. More than just a competition; it’s an experience that celebrates innovation, fosters creativity, and showcases the incredible talent within the technology landscape.
